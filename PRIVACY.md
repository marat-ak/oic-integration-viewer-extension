# OIC Integration Viewer — Privacy Policy

The OIC Integration Viewer Chrome extension does not collect, store on remote servers, or transmit any user data to third parties.

What stays on your device only:
- UI preferences (theme, color overrides, XPath preview length, last-used integration code/version) saved in chrome.storage.local on your computer.

Network requests:
- Same-origin GET requests to your authenticated Oracle Integration Cloud instance (under *.oraclecloud.com) — to search integrations and download the .iar archive of the integration you select. These requests are authenticated by your existing OIC session cookies; no separate credentials are stored or transmitted.

The extension contacts no third-party servers, analytics services, or telemetry endpoints.

For questions, please open an issue at:
https://github.com/marat-ak/oic-integration-viewer-extension/issues
